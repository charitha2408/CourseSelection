About Us

Welcome to the Student Course Selection Portal, a comprehensive platform designed to simplify academic course registration for students at KL Deemed to be University. Our goal is to make the student experience seamless, efficient, and user-friendly.

Our Vision

At KL Deemed to be University, we strive to be a global leader in higher education by fostering innovation, research, and excellence in teaching. Our portal reflects this commitment by enabling students to access their academic needs with just a few clicks.

Key Features of the Portal

  • Streamlined course registration for students.
  • Up-to-date timetables and academic schedules.
  • Real-time notifications about course updates.
  • Faculty tools for adding and editing courses with ease.
  • Feedback mechanism to ensure student and faculty satisfaction.

Our Legacy

KL Deemed to be University was established in 1980 as KL College of Engineering. Over the decades, we have evolved into a renowned educational institution, recognized as a Deemed to be University by the UGC in 2009.

Today, KL University is synonymous with academic excellence, offering students cutting-edge technology, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a dynamic learning environment.
